About Al-Rasheed Pharmacy

Our Story

A neighbourhood pharmacy built on trust

Al-Rasheed Pharmacy has been serving the families of Mian Channu with genuine medicines and honest advice for over a decade. What started as a small counter on G.T Road near Phatak Bazar has grown into one of the area's most trusted health destinations — without ever compromising on the values we started with.

Today, we stock everything from prescription medicines to premium skincare, supplements, baby care and diagnostic equipment — all sourced from licensed distributors, and all backed by our pharmacist's personal recommendation.
