Delivery Information

📍

Delivery Area

We currently deliver across Mian Channu city and nearby surrounding localities. WhatsApp us to confirm coverage for your exact address.

⏱️

Estimated Time

Most orders are delivered within 30–60 minutes of confirmation, depending on distance and order volume.

💰

Delivery Charges

A flat Rs 150 delivery fee applies to orders under Rs 1,500. Orders of Rs 1,500 or more qualify for free delivery.

🆓

Free Delivery Threshold

Spend Rs 1,500 or more in a single order and we'll waive the delivery fee entirely.

🧾

Order Preparation

Orders are typically packed and ready to dispatch within 10–15 minutes of confirmation.

🚨

Emergency Delivery

For urgent medicine needs, call us directly — we prioritise emergency orders any time of day or night.

🏬

Store Pickup

Prefer to collect yourself? Select "Store Pickup" at checkout and your order will be ready at our counter.

⚡

Express Delivery

Mark your order urgent in special instructions and we'll do our best to fast-track it ahead of the queue.
